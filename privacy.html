Information We Collect

Price Before Tax does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All calculations are performed locally on your device. We have no servers that receive your data, and we have no way to access any information you enter into the app.

Data Storage

Your saved tax rate presets are stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage system (UserDefaults). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Is not backed up to any external servers
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services. Your usage of the app is completely private. We don't know how often you use the app, what calculations you perform, or any other usage data.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services that could collect your data. There are no:

  • Analytics SDKs
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • External APIs or services

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages as it operates entirely offline and stores no personal data.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, your information benefits from the security measures built into iOS, including device encryption and secure storage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
